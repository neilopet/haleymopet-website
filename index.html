Evelyne Duskwood was raised to be poised, polished, and properly betrothed. But when courtly betrayal shatters her future and her younger brother begins to unravel under the weight of strange visions, Evelyne's carefully arranged life collapses—and something far darker begins to stir.

A scorched blood sigil. Whispers of a long-banished magic. A land ruled by shadows and fear.

Evelyne's search for answers pulls her north to Nerathar, a haunted territory twisted by blood magic and home to monsters that were once men. Among them: the Noskari, cursed beings who feed on power—and the enigmatic wolfpack that hunts them.

Captured and thrust into a world of instinct and secrecy, Evelyne finds herself at odds with Kaldrek, the pack's ruthless alpha. He's everything she resents: unbending, wild, and unmoved by her noble upbringing. But as danger grows and their paths entwine, the simmering tension between them ignites into something neither of them can deny.

In a land where magic is buried and blood remembers, Evelyne must confront long-hidden truths about her family, her heart, and a prophecy that could reshape the world.

About the Author

Haley M. Opet is a fantasy writer, mother, and dreamer who weaves tales of magic, intrigue, and adventure.

By day, I'm a mother of three, a wife, and a full-time professional who has built a career I'm proud of. But by night, I lose myself in the world of magic, intrigue, and adventure.

I've always been a lover of fantasy, drawn to rich worlds and characters that linger long after the last page. When a story took root in my mind—a tale of hidden magic, wolf shifters, and a heroine torn between duty and destiny—I knew I had to write it.

Now, I'm bringing that world to life, and I can't wait for you to step into the shadows with me.

Also, I'm a devoted pet lover—because every great fantasy world needs a loyal companion.
